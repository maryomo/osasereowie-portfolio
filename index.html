P.O.D web-application

This is the detail about the design thinking and development process of the print on demand web-application project..

My Role

As the designer in the team, I was responsible for carrying out research along with the product manager. We utilised the previous design system, while carrying out the redesign of the website.

Time-Line

This project took a total of 2 months to complete, from research to high fidelity design and hand over.

Background

At the start of the project we had minimal information about who we were redesiging for , we were tasked with carrying out detailed research on the persona, create the user journey map, figure out what was wrong with the present website design.

Due to budget limitations we could not carry out full research but had to utilise cheaper methods such as interviewing the customer service personnels to get information about customers correspondences, and this information we were able to know what customers pain points were, their complaints as well as their demography. We also made use of a tool called log rocket which enabled us see where customers were having difficulties as they navigated, as well as heat maps.

Goals & Limitation

    The ultimate goal of the project was to:

      know our customers better
      Reduce the user journey, so customers get to the checkout sooner
      HaveBetter intentionality with every feature
      Make the website More informational

The Design System

We made use of the previous design system, because we did not want to change the brand.

Kids Education Mobile App

This case study is to show the design thinking and development process of the kids education app project..

My Role

As a co designer in the team, I was responsible for carrying out research, and created the design system for the project, and i also created the Sketch component library & design templates.

Time-Line

This project took a total of 2 weeks to complete, from research to high fidelity design and hand over.

Background

We were provided with basic information about the persona we are to design the app for, we were tasked with carrying out a more detailed research on the persona, design a logo, user journey, create a design system which included the brand color.

We need to have an indepth understanding of who we are designing for at every stage of the design process, to ensure product success.

Goals & Limitation

    The ultimate goal of the project was to create a platform where kids would be able to learn, interact, share and get prompt responses from tutors. A learning support community.

This project was urgent which meant we had to deliver in the shortest time possible, therefore we had to work with less information as we normally would.

Building the Design System

Building up the design system is like building a house, you will need to build the foundation first, then gradually adding the bricks and concrete, and finally paint it in the beautiful colors you like.
